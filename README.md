# student-performance-analysis
A data-driven study of student performance metrics, featuring data cleaning, SQL querying, and interactive visualizations to improve academic insights.

🔹 Purpose

This dashboard provides a complete overview of student academic performance and highlights top-performing students.

📈 Key KPIs
Total Students: 10K
Pass Rate: 99.39%
High Performers: 590 students
Top 10 Percent Students: 1K students
📊 Visual Insights
👨‍🎓 High Performers by Gender
Shows the percentage of male and female students among high performers.
Male students slightly outperform female students.
🏆 Students by Grade

Displays the number of students in each grade category:

Grade C → Highest number of students
Grade B → Second highest
Grade A → Very limited top achievers
Grade F → Lowest count
🌟 Top 5 Students Overall Score

Highlights the top 5 students with the highest overall scores.

📚 Students by Extra Classes

Compares students who attended extra classes vs those who did not.

📈 Average Overall Score by Gender

Shows average academic performance between male and female students.

🥇 Top 10 Percent Students by Gender

Compares male and female students within the top 10% performers.

🎛 Filters/Slicers

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using:

Internet Access
Extra Classes
